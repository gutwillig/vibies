The mission

Six categories. One night. Every winner revealed live, with no embargoed press and no advance reveals — if you want to know who took home a Vibie, you tune in.

01

In production

Submissions point to real software with real users. Demos, prototypes, and unreleased projects do not qualify for consideration.

02

The Council

Founders, operators, and product leaders shortlist three finalists per category through a structured judging rubric.

03

The broadcast

Winners are revealed only on the night. Tune into the livestream live, or read the recap the morning after.

The schedule

Five milestones leading to the live broadcast on September 10. All times Pacific.

May 25

Submissions open

Eight weeks of open intake. Builders, teams, and fans can submit work.

Jul 20

Submissions close

Council convenes to begin shortlisting finalists across the six categories.

Aug 17

Finalists announced

Three finalists per category are announced. Public voting opens on the same day.

Sep 09

Voting closes

Final tallies locked overnight. The Council seals the envelope.

Sep 10

The Vibies, live

All six winners are revealed during the worldwide livestream.
